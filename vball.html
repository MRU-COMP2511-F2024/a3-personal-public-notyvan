Volleyball

This section will be showcasing my love for volleyball. Despite how short I am, I really enjoyed playing volleyball. Its competitiveness how the foundations can go really far, how both physically and mentally taxing it can be. There are just so many things about the sport that, in a way, takes off stress from responsibilities and real life in general.

Through volleyball, I learned many skills that not only can be used in the sport obviously, but as well as real life skills. Skills like leadership, confidence, persistence, too many to name, were all improved and gained through volleyball. Of course there are the sport-specific skills like agility, athleticsm, that I had to train in order to get even close to average. Being small, led to a brief imposter syndrome, as I've always dreamed to be an outside hitter but the coaches thought otherwise. I didn't want to be forced to play libero, although I did for one season, so I honed my skills in order to be a setter and as well as my jumping ability so that I would still be able to press my hands over the net.
